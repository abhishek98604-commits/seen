Seen.
A quiet exercise in looking.
This is an exercise in observation — exploring how much we notice compared to an AI trained to observe like an average person.

Spend a minute or two with the image. Notice the light, the mood, the subject, the small details that might otherwise slip by. Imagine the sounds, the stillness, the space. Then compare with how an AI observed the same moment.

There are no correct answers — only different ways of noticing.
